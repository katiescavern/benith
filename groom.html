Meet Ben

Ben is an adventurous soul who finds solace and excitement in conquering towering cliffs and challenging rock faces. Rock climbing has been a passion of his for years, and he has scaled breathtaking peaks in various parts of the world.

In addition to his love for rock climbing, Ben has a deep appreciation for history. He finds joy in exploring ancient ruins, reading historical accounts, and immersing himself in the stories of the past. He believes that understanding history allows us to learn from the experiences of those who came before us.

Ben's friends affectionately describe him as a "copium enthusiast."
