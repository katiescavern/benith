About Us

Ben and Zenith are deeply in love and excited to start this new chapter in their lives together. They first crossed paths on a serendipitous day at Inquiry Hub Secondary. Their eyes met, and an instant connection sparked between them. It was as if the universe had conspired to bring them together.

Since that fateful meeting, Benith has embarked on a beautiful journey filled with love, laughter, and countless cherished memories. They have explored the world hand in hand, from romantic getaways in quaint European cities to adventurous hikes in breathtaking natural landscapes. Together, they have celebrated birthdays, anniversaries, and everyday joys, creating a tapestry of shared experiences.

Their love is built on a foundation of unwavering support, understanding, and mutual respect. They have weathered life's challenges together, standing as pillars of strength for each other. In each other's embrace, they have found solace, encouragement, and the courage to chase their dreams.

Benith's journey has been shaped by their shared passions and interests. They are avid food enthusiasts, often seeking out hidden culinary gems to indulge in delicious gastronomic adventures. They also have a shared love for outdoor activities, whether it's cycling along scenic routes or strolling hand in hand through sun-kissed parks.

As they stand on the precipice of matrimony, Ben and Zenith express their deep gratitude to their families and friends who have supported and celebrated their love story. They eagerly look forward to their wedding day, where they can exchange vows, surrounded by the warmth and love of their loved ones, and embark on the next chapter of their extraordinary journey together as Benith.
